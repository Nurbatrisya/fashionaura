Frequently Asked Questions

What is FashionAura's return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase, provided the items are in their original condition with all tags attached.

How long does shipping take?

Shipping typically takes 5-7 business days for standard delivery and 2-3 business days for express delivery.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. Shipping charges and times vary based on location.

Can I cancel or modify my order?

You can cancel or modify your order within 24 hours of placing it. Contact our support team for assistance.

How do I track my order?

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive an email with the tracking number and link to track your package.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards such as Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and PayPal. Additionally, we also support bank transfers and cash on delivery in selected regions.

Are your products ethically made?

Yes, we are committed to ethical production practices. Our products are crafted by skilled artisans under fair working conditions.

How do I take care of my FashionAura items?

Each item comes with specific care instructions. Generally, we recommend hand washing or using a delicate cycle with cold water and air drying.

Do you offer gift cards?

Yes, we offer digital gift cards in various denominations. They are perfect for any occasion and can be redeemed online.

Can I customize or personalize my order?

We offer limited customization options on select items. Please contact our support team for more information.

What do I do if I receive a damaged item?

If you receive a damaged item, please contact our customer service team within 7 days of delivery with a photo of the damage for assistance.

Do you have a loyalty program?

Yes, we have a loyalty program where you can earn points for every purchase, which can be redeemed for discounts on future orders.

How do I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support team via email, phone, or our Contact Us form on the website. Our support hours are Monday to Friday, 9 AM - 5 PM.

Do you offer discounts for bulk purchases?

Yes, we offer special discounts for bulk orders. Please contact our sales team for further details and pricing.

How do I unsubscribe from the newsletter?

You can unsubscribe by clicking the "Unsubscribe" link at the bottom of any newsletter email you receive from us.
